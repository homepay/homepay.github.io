our progress

How we see our growth

Discover the milestones shaping our journey towards revolutionizing the financial landscape.

  • 2022

    Launched Bangladesh's first real-time remittance platform Blaze

    In the third quarter of 2021, HomePay launched Blaze, a 24/7/365 real-time remittance service connecting to 42 banks in Bangladesh. Our partners began testing Blaze towards the end of 2021 and commenced full-scale operations at the start of 2022

  • 2023

    Remittance gateway for Local partners

    In the first quarter of 2023, we introduced the remittance gateway, enabling local partners to disburse remittances through various channels such as Mobile Financial Services (MFS) and the Instant Bank network.

  • 2024

    National card scheme directory TakaPay

    At the end of 2023, Bangladesh Bank conducted a soft launch of the National Card Scheme, TakaPay, with plans for a commercial launch in the second quarter of 2024. HomePay is a key partner of Bangladesh Bank, providing TakaPay's Directory Service to facilitate card-not-present transactions.
