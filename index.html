Real-Time Cross-Border
Platform Blaze

  • Blaze stands as instant cross-border payment network, operating seamlessly every hour of every day, throughout the entire year. This groundbreaking service fosters unparalleled connectivity between global remittance entities and an extensive network of over 40 banks in Bangladesh, revolutionizing the way cross-border transactions are conducted.

  • In the third quarter of 2021, Blaze launched as a 24/7/365 real-time remittance service connecting to 42 banks in Bangladesh. Our partners began testing Blaze towards the end of 2021 and commenced full-scale operations at the start of 2022.

Remittance Gateway For Local Partners

  • Leveraging Blaze's robust remittance infrastructure, we engineered a gateway specifically tailored to the rapidly expanding Mobile Financial Service (MFS) sector in the country. This bespoke solution is designed to meet the unique needs of banks and foreign Money Transfer Operators (MTOs), enabling them to provide real-time remittance services with unprecedented efficiency and reliability.

  • In the first quarter of 2023, the remittance gateway was launched, enabling local partners to disburse remittances through various channels such as Mobile Financial Services (MFS) and the Instant Bank network.

TakaPay Card Authentication

  • The TakaPay Directory, developed exclusively for Bangladesh Bank's TakaPay project, is tailored to facilitate 3D Secure (3DS) transactions within the National Card Scheme "TakaPay," adhering to EMVCo Standards for optimal security and compliance. This solution authenticates cardholders for card-not-present transactions, crucial for modern e-commerce and banking, ensuring robust fraud mitigation and enhancing user trust. It supports TakaPay's goal of secure, compliant, and efficient electronic payments, bolstering Bangladesh's digital financial infrastructure.

  • At the end of 2023, Bangladesh Bank conducted a soft launch of the National Card Scheme, TakaPay, with plans for a commercial launch in the second quarter of 2024. HomePay is a key partner of Bangladesh Bank, providing TakaPay's Directory Service to facilitate card-not-present transactions.
