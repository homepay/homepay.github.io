Blaze

Blaze stands as Bangladesh's pioneering instant cross-border payment network, operating seamlessly every hour of every day, throughout the entire year. This groundbreaking service fosters connectivity between global remittance entities and an extensive network of over 40 banks in Bangladesh.

Learn More

Nagad Remittance

Leveraging Blaze's robust remittance infrastructure, we engineered an exclusive remittance gateway tailored for the country's most rapidly expanding Mobile Financial Service (MFS), Nagad. This bespoke solution enables real-time remittance services for banks and foreign Money Transfer Operators (MTOs), facilitating seamless and efficient cross-border transactions.

Learn More

TakaPay Directory

The TakaPay Directory is a bespoke solution exclusively developed for the TakaPay project, owned by Bangladesh Bank. Tailored to meet the specific needs of the project, it serves as a specialized tool for facilitating 3D Secure (3DS) transactions within the framework of the National Card Scheme "TakaPay," adhering strictly to EMVCo Standards. This exclusive solution is designed to facilitate secure and compliant electronic payments within the framework of the TakaPay initiative.

our progress

How we see our growth

Discover the milestones shaping our journey towards revolutionizing the financial landscape.

  • 2022

    Launched Bangladesh's first real-time remittance platform Blaze

    In the third quarter of 2021, HomePay launched Blaze, a 24/7/365 real-time remittance service connecting to 42 banks in Bangladesh. Our partners began testing Blaze towards the end of 2021 and commenced full-scale operations at the start of 2022

  • 2023

    Exclusive remittance gateway for the country's fastest growing MFS Nagad

    In the first quarter of 2023, Nagad, the country's fastest-growing mobile financial service (MFS), launched their remittance service, exclusively partnering with HomePay as their remittance technology provider

  • 2024

    National card scheme directory TakaPay

    At the end of 2023, Bangladesh Bank conducted a soft launch of the National Card Scheme, TakaPay, with plans for a commercial launch in the second quarter of 2024. HomePay is a key partner of Bangladesh Bank, providing TakaPay's Directory Service to facilitate card-not-present transactions.
